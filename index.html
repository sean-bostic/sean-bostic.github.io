Hello World, I am Sean Bostic

I am someone who has been fascinated with computers since I was a kid. I've been a software developer for about 5 years and studied at the Univeristy of Cincinnati. This is my personal website which I occasionally update with a blog post or project update.


I always enjoy problem solving and using critical thinking to solve complex problems. I have had the most fun doing this with the almost limitless power of software. Ever since I was a kid, I always enjoyed working with computers. I started out with hardware believe it or not and eventually found a career in software development. I built my first computer from scratch when I was about ten which really was just a shiny new computer case with salvaged parts from various other machines that I had disassembled. I thoroughly enjoyed building a computer through my young adult years and it eventually led me into the software side of computing.
